Specials

Balinese Sambal Matah (Raw Sambal)

A spicy condiment made from raw shallots, lemongrass, chili, lime, and coconut oil, often served with grilled fish or chicken for a zesty kick.

Nasi Campur Bali (Mixed Rice)

A plate of white rice served with various side dishes like meat, vegetables, peanuts, and sambal. It's a versatile dish that showcases a variety of Balinese flavors.

Sate Lilit (Minced Meat Skewers)

A Balinese take on satay, using minced fish, chicken, or pork mixed with coconut, spices, and herbs, wrapped around lemongrass sticks and grilled.

Lawar

A mix of vegetables, coconut, minced meat, and Balinese spices. It can include green beans, grated coconut, and even blood for an authentic flavor.

Ayam Betutu (Spiced Chicken)

A famous Balinese dish where chicken is marinated in a rich blend of spices and wrapped in banana leaves, then slow-cooked for tender, flavorful meat.
